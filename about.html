About Home Maintenance Plus

Andre Dicaire is the owner of Home Maintenance Plus. He founded the business in 1996 but has over 30+ years of experience with all aspects of home renovation and commercial construction. Andre is able to design, with his Graphic Arts background, different layouts and designs. Based in Ottawa, Home Maintenance Plus works specifically in Ottawa but not Quebéc. Andre's specialties are basements, brilliant and elegant work in designing washrooms, painting, flooring, drywall, electrical, plumbing and moulding. All other sub trades are done by a team of expert sub contractors, whom work closely with each other to give you the best work.

All work done in your home will fulfill your expectations. For examples of work and current renovations, visit any of the pages to the left to view various images of work.
